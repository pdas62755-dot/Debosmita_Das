Our History

For over three decades, 100SMILES Dental Care has been proudly serving the Razole community. We offer a comprehensive range of services from preventive check-ups and cleanings to orthodontics, implants, and cosmetic dentistry. Our focus on patient education ensures informed decisions for lifelong dental health.

Our Technologies

We integrate cutting-edge technologies such as Digital X-Rays, Intraoral Cameras, Laser Dentistry, CAD/CAM systems, 3D printing, and Teledentistry to provide precise, comfortable, and convenient dental care for all patients.

Online Booking & Special Offers

Our online booking system allows you to schedule appointments easily. We also offer special promotions, including free dental checkups and discounts on annual dental visits to enhance your experience with us.
